What Are Contrails?

Contrails (condensation trails) are ice-crystal clouds formed in aircraft engine exhaust at high altitudes. They can persist for hours and spread into cirrus-like clouds that trap heat in the atmosphere.

Contrails may contribute more to aviation warming than CO₂ itself.
✈️

Why Optimize Routes?

Using AI to detect contrail-forming zones enables airlines to:

  • 📍 Identify contrail-heavy airspace regions
  • ✈️ Reroute flights to avoid persistent contrail creation
  • 📉 Reduce aviation's climate forcing by up to 54%
  • 💰 Achieve this with only ~0.3% additional fuel cost
